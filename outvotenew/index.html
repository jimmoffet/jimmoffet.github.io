Friends don't let friends not vote.

Outvote is an app for getting your friends to vote. Outvote exists because your friends don't vote. Most of them want to, but they don't. They get busy. It's hard to make time. Friends that get reminders are twice as likely to vote. Outvote makes it easy to remind your friends to register and vote.

Android Iphone

What is Outvote?

Outvote helps you match your friends with the causes and candidates that are right for them. Because in a world filled with clickbait, fake news, and Russian bots, that's what friends are for.

A Better Way to Reach Out

Convincing strangers to take action is hard. Convincing your friends isn't. Contacts messaged through Outvote are twice as likely to vote.

Learn More

Flip an Election this November

Outvote allows you to plug into Democratic campaigns as a digital activist. We find your friends that are registered to vote in the district and let you use scripts provided by the campaign to reach out.

Testimonials

Kind words from our users

Team Outvote played an important role in my first campaign. As a new candidate, I had to respect tried-and-true canvassing strategies, but also wanted to test digital tools that make it easy for people to reach out to their friends and family. I knew the race would be close and had to rely on the strength of my base. Outvote was another way to reach those people. It's an innovative tool, intuitive, and easy to learn. They were always available to answer any questions and even trained our volunteers and canvassers.

Sumbul Siddiqui
Cambridge City Counselor

Outvote helped our campaign approach voter outreach in an entirely new way. In the weeks leading up to election day, the campaign was able to reach more potential voters and expand our network in a targeted and productive manner. The Outvote team was extremely flexible and took the time to ensure each of our supporters understood how to use the service, regardless of technical ability. Our supporters enjoyed bringing new people into the political process and reconnecting with old friends along the way. On election day, turnout was extremely high and Quinton won a seat on the Cambridge City Council.

Quinton Zondervan
Cambridge City Counselor
