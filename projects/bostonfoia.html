Boston FOIA Tech Stack Redesign

Tech and Innovation in Government with Nick Sinai

I am currently consulting under Nick Sinai at Kennedy School of Government on a redesign of Boston’s FOIA request experience. In my role as UX manager, I work closely with the product manager to delegate user research and prototype design tasks across a team of 5 researchers, designers, devs and policy analysts.

The project involves scoping a product development project in conversation with the City of Boston; conducting user research in the field; rapidly designing, building, and testing lightweight prototypes; and creating a plan to develop and roll out features through modular contracting. The team is also providing policy, operations, and strategy recommendations.
