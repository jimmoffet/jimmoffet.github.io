Open Civic Engagement ChatBot

Simple Extensible SMS Chat Platform

Visit my github repository to download or fork this project.


With funding from the Harvard School of Design Service Fellowship, I developed a piece of software for strengthening civic engagement, one which allows civic organizations to easily pull data from government websites and deliver it to their constituencies via low-touch, high-engagement channels, such as SMS.

This app is free-to-use, open-source and has been developed to be as simple to deploy as possible, so that civic and community groups without dedicated IT teams can launch and maintain it. The intended audience for this app is any civic/community group that has access to temporary or volunteer technical labor.

While the first incarnation of the app notifies users of upcoming city council meetings, the possibilities for deploying the software are quite broad. Any information that is published to the web can be obtained by the app and delivered to the users, and the chat logic is only limited by the organizer’s imagination.
